33" scratching post

Solid wood construction with replaceable sisal fiber rope. The front scratching post has a slight curve to help your cat stretch out and claw away.

Legs are easily removed and secured strong enough to hold very heavy cats. I have pressed all my weight on the top to stress test it. It is built to last!

Dimensions:

33 1/2" high (floor to top)
16" square top
24 1/2" wide (outer edges of the back legs)
29 1/2" from the back edge to the bottom front of the scratching post.

This was built in a cat free environment to ensure your home owner will be the first to mark it as their personal piece.
